About

Two modes. One sensibility.

XX

I work in two modes: commercial and authorial. In one, I build sound worlds for games, films, and brands. In the other, I build them for myself.

Both come from the same place — a belief that audio isn't just heard, it's felt. Whether I'm mixing a Dolby Atmos film score or producing an electronic-indie track, the goal is the same: make it move people.
